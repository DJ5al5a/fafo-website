Who We Are

Friends and Family Outreach (FAFO) is a community-led advocacy and resource organization, founded by parents and families who have personally experienced abuse, misconduct, or unlawful actions by government agencies involved in child and family systems.

Important: We Are NOT Lawyers

FAFO does NOT provide legal advice or legal representation. We are parents and community members who have lived through this system.

Our Purpose

  • Share plain-language explanations of laws and policies
  • Help families understand their basic rights
  • Provide official complaint pathways and resources
  • Point people to legitimate help when they have nowhere else to turn

This site is built by the people this happened to, for people currently going through it.

Our Mission

🛡️

Protect Families

Protect parents and families from unlawful government overreach and ensure agencies follow the law.

🔍

Promote Transparency

Promote transparency and accountability in child welfare systems and government agencies.

📚

Educate & Empower

Educate families so they are not isolated, confused, or powerless when facing the system.

Who This Site Is For

This website is for parents and families in Florida who are:

  • Currently under investigation by DCF (Department of Children and Families)
  • Involved in dependency court proceedings
  • Separated from their children by the child welfare system
  • Experiencing misconduct or unlawful actions by child welfare agencies
  • Trying to understand their rights and the legal process
  • Looking for resources, support, and guidance

If you're scared, overwhelmed, and don't know where to turn — this resource is for you. We remember having nothing and no one. We created this so you don't have to face this alone.

Why This Resource Exists

When government agencies have the power to separate families, transparency and accountability aren't optional — they're essential.

Too many families face these systems alone, confused, and powerless. They don't know their rights. They don't understand the process. They don't know where to turn for help or how to hold agencies accountable when procedures aren't followed.

We built this site because we remember what it felt like to have nothing and no one.

FAFO exists to ensure families have access to clear, honest information about their rights, the legal process, and legitimate pathways for accountability. Knowledge is power, and no family should face this system without understanding it.
