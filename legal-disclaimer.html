Who We Are

Friends and Family Outreach (FAFO) is a community-led advocacy and resource organization founded by parents and families who have experienced the child welfare system. We provide:

  • Plain-language explanations of laws and policies
  • Educational resources about parental rights
  • Information about complaint pathways and accountability
  • Referrals to legitimate legal aid and support resources

We do NOT provide:

  • Legal advice or legal representation
  • Case-specific counsel or guidance
  • Court representation or advocacy
  • Financial assistance or direct services

If You're Facing a Legal Issue

Get Professional Legal Help
